### 8.10.2. Fence-SC


_Fence-SC_ order cannot contradict _causality order_. For a pair of _morally strong_ _fence.sc_ operations F1 and F2, if F1 precedes F2 in _causality order_ , then F1 must precede F2 in _Fence-SC_ order.
